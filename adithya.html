Adithya Paramasivam

The Man

Adithya Paramasivam is an eleventh grade student at High Technology High School in Lincroft, NJ.


Among his friends, Adithya P. is known as an excellent programmer and a longtime participant in the VEX robotics competition.


Adithya P. was recently chosen for such prestigious recognitions as the High Tech MathWorks Modeling Challenge A Team and the 2018 Jersey Shore Junior Science Symposium poster session.


Naturally, such an accomplished fellow and handsome man attracts a lot of attention from his classmates. He is known to be a much sought-after friend and very popular with the ladies.

The Myth

The Legend

After reading about this incredible man, one would be forgiven for thinking that he is but a myth. But he is not. Adithya P. is a very real man who has done a lot of pretty incredible things. But he needs your help.
